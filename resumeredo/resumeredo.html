Myla Miranda

An aspiring artist seeking opportunities express to her passion for creativity while providing exceptional products for clientele. Myla (Marks) Miranda is currently studying to receive her Associate of Arts degree from Seminole State College with the intention of transferring to the Univeristy of Central Florida to complete her Bachelors degree in graphic design and illustration. Along with school she works as a freelance designer creating logos, business cards, invitations and other print media works commissioned by friends and outside clientele. After receiving her degree, Myla intends on pursuing a career as a freelance designer and illustrator with a strong focus on print media and illustrations for childrens books. She has previously interned as a graphic designer/marketing assistant and continues to look for opportunites to enhance her skills in the Adobe Suite products, gain experience in the field of design and build her portfolio.
